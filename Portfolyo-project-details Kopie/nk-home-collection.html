THE CLIENT

NK Home Collection is a premium home décor and furniture retailer offering curated collections of modern and classic pieces. The company specializes in high-quality home furnishings, decorative items, and lifestyle products. With a focus on elegant design and customer satisfaction, NK Home Collection serves customers seeking sophisticated interior solutions for their homes.

02

THE CHALLENGE

NK Home Collection needed a custom e-commerce platform to showcase their extensive product catalog and provide a seamless online shopping experience. The challenge included integrating secure payment processing with Troy payment gateway, implementing robust authentication and authorization systems, and maintaining visual consistency across diverse product photography from multiple suppliers. The platform needed to handle inventory management, order processing, and provide an intuitive shopping experience while ensuring transaction security.

E-Commerce Secure Payment Custom Development Product Management
03

THE SOLUTION

I developed a custom e-commerce platform using WordPress with a fully customized theme, integrating WooCommerce for product and order management. The solution features secure Troy payment gateway integration with additional custom security layers implementing Authentication & Authorization protocols to protect customer transactions. I standardized all product photography using Photoshop, ensuring consistent angles and presentation across the entire catalog despite images coming from various suppliers. The platform includes custom product filtering, wishlist functionality, secure checkout process, and responsive design optimized for all devices. Advanced inventory management and order tracking systems provide both customers and administrators with real-time updates.
