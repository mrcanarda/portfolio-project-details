THE CLIENT

U-BER Pulverbeschichtung is a specialized powder coating (Pulverbeschichtung) service provider based in Detmold, Germany, offering comprehensive metal surface treatment and coating solutions. The company specializes in powder coating, an industrial process that creates protective and aesthetic surfaces on metal through electrostatic powder application and high-temperature curing. U-BER provides professional services including Pulverbeschichtung (powder coating with electrostatic application for corrosion and weather resistance), Entlackung (safe removal of existing coatings), Elektrostatische Lackierung (electrostatic paint application), Phosphatierung (phosphate pre-treatment for better adhesion), and Abkleben/Maskierung (precision masking for detailed work). The company serves multiple industries including Architecture & Construction (balconies, fences, railings), Automotive & Transportation (vehicle parts, trailers, carriers), Industrial & Machinery (equipment components), and Furniture & Garden Equipment (metal furniture and outdoor fixtures).

02

THE CHALLENGE

U-BER Pulverbeschichtung needed a professional website to showcase their specialized powder coating services and reach clients across multiple industrial sectors. The challenge was effectively communicating technical coating processes to diverse audiences from construction to automotive industries, presenting five distinct service offerings clearly, demonstrating expertise in industrial metal treatment, establishing credibility in the competitive coating industry, and accommodating specific client preferences including unique design requirements such as adjusted line heights that, while unconventional, reflected the client's vision. The website needed to balance technical professionalism with accessibility for clients from various industries who may not be familiar with powder coating terminology.

WordPress Development Industrial Services Metal Treatment Multi-Industry Platform
03

THE SOLUTION

I developed a comprehensive website using WordPress that effectively presents U-BER Pulverbeschichtung's specialized services and industry expertise. The solution features detailed service sections covering all five core offerings (Pulverbeschichtung, Entlackung, Elektrostatische Lackierung, Phosphatierung, Abkleben), clear explanations of technical processes in accessible language, industry-specific pages targeting Architecture/Construction, Automotive/Transportation, Industrial/Machinery, and Furniture/Garden sectors, professional presentation of metal coating capabilities, and contact information with location details in Detmold, Germany. The WordPress platform provides easy content management for service updates, responsive design for accessibility across devices, and professional layout showcasing industrial expertise. While the design includes unique specifications such as custom line heights per client preference, the website successfully communicates U-BER's technical capabilities and serves as an effective platform for attracting clients across multiple industrial sectors. The site emphasizes both protective and decorative metal surface improvements, positioning U-BER as a comprehensive solution provider for industrial metal coating needs.
