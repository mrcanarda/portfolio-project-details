THE CLIENT

EKA Architecture is an Istanbul-based architecture and engineering firm offering modern architectural and engineering solutions. The company provides services across a wide range from urban transformation projects to custom residential designs. With a professional team and innovative approach, they are a leader in the sector.

02

THE CHALLENGE

EKA Architecture needed a modern, professional, and user-friendly corporate website. The website had to effectively showcase the company's services, projects, and latest developments. A minimalist, high-quality, and focused design approach was required.

Web Design Custom Development
03

THE SOLUTION

The website, developed with modern, responsive design and glassmorphism principles, reflects EKA Architecture's professional identity. A custom-coded project filtering system allows users to easily browse projects by category—residential, commercial, ongoing projects, and completed works. User experience was optimized with features like dynamic project gallery, services section, and contact form. The responsive design ensures a flawless appearance across all devices.
