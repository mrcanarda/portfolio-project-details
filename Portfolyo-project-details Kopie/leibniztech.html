THE CLIENT

LeibnizTech is a digital technology and creative agency and subsidiary of Route Grup, operating from two locations in Willich, Germany and Izmir, Turkey. As part of the Route Grup family alongside RouteVera, the company positions itself as "a full-service digital technology company offering comprehensive solutions in SEO, content marketing, web design, and software development that grow brands." With 5+ years of professional experience and 25+ completed projects, LeibnizTech provides digital support to brands across small, medium, and enterprise businesses. The company delivers integrated solutions combining Technology + Design + Strategy, offering four core service categories: UI/UX Experience (user-friendly, professional interface design and UX-focused prototyping), Web Development (modern, responsive, performance-oriented web solutions), Dashboard Development (custom analytics panels, reporting systems, and business intelligence software), and Digital Marketing (SEO optimization, content marketing, and brand visibility campaigns). LeibnizTech serves clients seeking comprehensive digital transformation, from web presence to marketing strategy, leveraging the broader expertise of the Route Grup network.

02

THE CHALLENGE

LeibnizTech needed a professional platform to showcase their comprehensive digital services and establish credibility as a technology and creative agency operating across Germany and Turkey. The challenge was effectively presenting four distinct service categories to diverse client segments, communicating expertise across both technical development and creative marketing, showcasing dual-location capability in Germany and Turkey, establishing authority with 5+ years experience and 25+ projects, appealing to small businesses, medium enterprises, and corporate clients simultaneously, and demonstrating the integrated Technology + Design + Strategy approach. The website needed to reflect modern digital agency standards while communicating LeibnizTech's unique value proposition in competitive German and Turkish markets.

Digital Agency UI/UX Design Web Development Digital Marketing
03

THE SOLUTION

I designed and developed a comprehensive digital agency website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with fully custom design and coding. The solution features dedicated service sections for UI/UX Experience showcasing user-friendly interface design and UX-focused prototyping capabilities, Web Development highlighting modern, responsive, performance-oriented solutions, Dashboard Development presenting custom analytics panels and business intelligence software, and Digital Marketing detailing SEO, content marketing, and brand visibility services. The website prominently displays company achievements including 5+ years of professional experience, 25+ completed projects, and dual locations in Willich, Germany and Izmir, Turkey. Professional presentation includes the core mission statement emphasizing brand growth through comprehensive digital services, the Technology + Design + Strategy integrated approach, project portfolio showcasing work across all service categories, client testimonials and case studies, team expertise and capabilities, and clear service descriptions for each offering. The custom-coded solution provides optimal performance with hand-written HTML structure, custom CSS styling without frameworks, vanilla JavaScript for interactions, responsive design for all devices, smooth animations and transitions, professional agency aesthetics, and clean, maintainable code. The design positions LeibnizTech as a modern digital partner with technical expertise, serving clients across both European and Turkish markets with a platform that demonstrates the agency's own development capabilities.
