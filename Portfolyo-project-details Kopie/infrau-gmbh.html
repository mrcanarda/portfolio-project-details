THE CLIENT

Infrau GmbH is a German construction company founded in 2020, specializing in infrastructure projects for government and municipal clients. The company focuses on road construction, bridge projects, and public buildings including government libraries and infrastructure facilities. With an impressive track record of over 2,500 completed projects, 1,500+ satisfied clients, 4,500 employed workers, and 260 awards won, Infrau GmbH has established itself as a leading construction partner for public sector projects across Germany. The company's expertise lies in high-quality construction, precision work, and timely project delivery for critical infrastructure development.

02

THE CHALLENGE

Infrau GmbH needed a professional corporate website that reflects their authority and expertise in government and municipal construction projects. The challenge was creating a platform that showcases their impressive portfolio and statistics while maintaining the professional, trustworthy image required for public sector work. The website needed to effectively present their extensive project history, demonstrate capability for large-scale infrastructure projects, communicate reliability and quality standards required for government work, showcase their awards and achievements, and provide clear information for potential municipal and government clients. The design had to balance modern web aesthetics with the conservative, professional expectations of public sector clients.

Custom Design Infrastructure Projects Corporate Identity Government Sector
03

THE SOLUTION

I designed and developed a custom corporate website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, creating a professional platform that establishes credibility and showcases Infrau GmbH's capabilities. The solution features a bold, impactful hero section with the tagline "WIR BAUEN IHRE ZUKUNFT" (We Build Your Future) and striking construction imagery. Key statistics are prominently displayed including 2,500+ completed projects, 1,500+ satisfied clients, 4,500 employed workers, and 260 awards won. The website includes comprehensive sections covering services (Hochbau, Restaurierung, Infrastruktur), ongoing projects showcase, company information and history, career opportunities section, and contact information with phone and email. The design uses a professional color scheme with yellow accents representing construction and infrastructure, responsive layout ensuring accessibility across all devices, and smooth navigation between service categories and project portfolios. The custom-coded solution provides optimal performance while maintaining the professional appearance essential for government sector clients.
