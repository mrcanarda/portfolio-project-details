THE CLIENT

RouteVera is an international consulting firm and subsidiary of Route Grup, specializing in providing comprehensive guidance for individuals seeking opportunities in Germany. The company offers expert consulting services for education pathways, employment opportunities, and business establishment in Germany. With a focus on making international relocation seamless, RouteVera guides clients through every step of their journey to Germany, from initial planning to successful settlement. The company serves Turkish-speaking clients looking to pursue education, career advancement, or entrepreneurship in the German market.

02

THE CHALLENGE

RouteVera needed a professional, trustworthy website to showcase their consulting services and establish credibility in the international consulting market. The challenge was creating a platform that effectively communicates complex immigration, education, and business processes in an accessible way while maintaining a professional corporate identity. The website needed to present multiple service categories clearly, build trust with potential clients, provide comprehensive information about Germany opportunities, and facilitate easy contact and consultation booking. As a subsidiary of Route Grup, the design also needed to align with corporate branding while establishing RouteVera's distinct identity.

Figma Design Corporate Identity Consulting Services International Business
03

THE SOLUTION

I designed and developed a custom corporate website using Figma for the design phase and implementing it with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The solution features a clean, professional design that builds trust and credibility, comprehensive service pages detailing education consulting, employment guidance, and business establishment support, an about page showcasing RouteVera's expertise and connection to Route Grup, a contact page with inquiry forms for consultation requests, and responsive design ensuring optimal viewing across all devices. The website uses a green color scheme reflecting growth and new opportunities, with airplane imagery symbolizing international journey and transition. Smooth animations and transitions enhance user experience, while clear calls-to-action guide visitors toward consultation booking. The site architecture allows easy navigation between service categories and information sections.
