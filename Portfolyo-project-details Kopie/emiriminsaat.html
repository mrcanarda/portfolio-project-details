THE CLIENT

Emirim İnşaat is an Istanbul-based construction company founded by Cabil Bildik, specializing in engineering-based contracting, residential projects, and urban transformation initiatives. With 8 years of industry experience and over 500 completed projects, the company has established itself as a reliable construction partner in Turkey's competitive market. Emirim İnşaat's comprehensive services include residential projects and building construction, urban transformation (kentsel dönüşüm) initiatives, turnkey contracting services, commercial and industrial structures, prefabricated and stone houses, landscape and environmental design, and infrastructure works. The company combines engineering expertise with quality construction practices, serving clients across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors with a focus on urban renewal and modern construction solutions.

02

THE CHALLENGE

Emirim İnşaat needed a professional corporate website to showcase their extensive construction portfolio and establish credibility in Istanbul's competitive construction market. The challenge was effectively presenting eight distinct service categories to diverse client segments, showcasing 500+ completed projects and 8 years of experience, communicating expertise in both residential and commercial construction, highlighting specialization in urban transformation projects, establishing trust for large-scale contracting work, and creating a platform that appeals to individual homeowners, commercial clients, and municipal urban transformation partners. The website needed to reflect the company's engineering foundation and quality-focused approach while making their diverse services easily accessible to potential clients.

WordPress Development Urban Transformation Turnkey Projects Engineering Excellence
03

THE SOLUTION

I developed a comprehensive corporate website using WordPress with custom design that effectively presents Emirim İnşaat's capabilities and portfolio. The solution features dedicated service sections covering residential projects and building construction, urban transformation initiatives, turnkey contracting services, commercial and industrial structures, prefabricated and stone houses, landscape and environmental design, and infrastructure works. The website prominently displays company achievements including 8 years of industry experience and 500+ completed projects, founder information featuring Cabil Bildik, and Istanbul location and service areas. Professional presentation elements include project portfolio showcasing completed works across all service categories, testimonials and client success stories, company credentials and certifications, detailed service descriptions for each construction category, and clear calls-to-action for project inquiries and consultations. The custom-designed WordPress platform provides easy content management for project updates, responsive design for accessibility across all devices, professional layout reflecting engineering expertise, and Turkish language content for local market. The design balances corporate professionalism with approachability, positioning Emirim İnşaat as a comprehensive construction solution provider capable of handling projects from individual residences to large-scale urban transformation initiatives.
